I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science at Peking University, advised by Associate Professor (with tenure) [Guojie Luo](http://ceca.pku.edu.cn/people/alumni/guojie__luo/index.htm) at [PKU-DASYS Group](http://ceca.pku.edu.cn/dasys/) (Design Automation for Next-generation Computing Systems). My current research interests include computer architecture, electronic design automation (EDA), AI systems, and sketch-based computing. I am exploring how to enhance domain-specific system performance and efficiency through both compilation techniques and architecture design.

I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science in 2022 from the School of EECS at Peking University. I have been affiliated with the PKU-DASYS Group with Professor Guojie Luo since 2019. During my undergraduate studies and Ph.D. pursuit, I have focused on logic optimization, high-level synthesis, and domain-specific accelerator design.
